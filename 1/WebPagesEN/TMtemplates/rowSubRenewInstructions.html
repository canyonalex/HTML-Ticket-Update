When you renew, all tickets in the subscription are added to your shopping cart.

You may request a change in the seats or date and we will do our best to accommodate your request after the renewal deadline. If your change can be accommodated, the difference in the cost due to a change of low(D_SEAT_DESC), low(D_SECTION_DESC), or low(D_PERF_DESC), will be charged or refunded to the credit card used to renew the subscription.
