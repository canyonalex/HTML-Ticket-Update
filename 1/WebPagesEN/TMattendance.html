This screen is used to check a low(D_PATRON) into and/or out of your low(D_VENUE) for a low(D_PERF_DESC).
You can scan the ticket number from the ticket (or type it in) the field and then click the:
  • 'Check D_PATRON In' -or-
  • 'Check D_PATRON Out'

button depending on what you are trying to accomplish.

You do not need to use this page you are using a Symbol MC50/MC55 Wireless device to track attendance.
