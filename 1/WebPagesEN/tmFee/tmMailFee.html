Mailing Fee

F_HTML_PAGE_MESSAGE

Mailing fees are charged to cover the costs of sending you your tickets and/or passes in the mail. Tickets and Passes are mailed via 1st Class Postage. Delivery may take 7 to 10 business days to arrive.

It is important to note that no tickets will be mailed for performances within 10 days of the purchase date.

D_COMPANY_LONG is not responsible for lost or stolen tickets in the mail.
